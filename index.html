Donate for Flood at Noakhali, Bangladesh

Natural disasters remind us of our shared humanity. Recently, the picturesque region of Noakhali in Bangladesh has been struck by devastating floods, leaving thousands of families homeless and struggling to survive. As we witness their plight, we have a chance to make a meaningful difference..

Donate for Flood Relief in Feni,Bangladesh

Continuous torrential rains have caused rivers in Feni to overflow, inundating villages, towns, and farmlands. Thousands of families are now homeless, with limited access to food, clean water, and medical supplies. Schools and markets are closed, livelihoods are disrupted, and recovery seems like a distant dream for many.

Aid for Injured in the Quota Movement

The Quota Reform Movement began as a call for equity in government job recruitment, demanding a reduction in the quota system's disproportionate allocation. The movement, largely peaceful, met with violent crackdowns, leaving many protesters injured and in need of medical assistance. These individuals, many of them students, sacrificed their safety for the dream of a more equitable future. It’s our responsibility to ensure they don’t bear the aftermath alone
